Founded in 1994 by Mike Meldman,Discovery Land Company is
a US-based real estate developer and operator of private residential
club communities and resorts with a world-renowed portfolio of
domestic and international properties.

Our Mission

We dont build buildings,but rather we build settings
where families can create intergenerational memories
and enrich their lives in exquisite and untouched
sanctuaries.Everything we do is driven by the
fundamental desires to create spaces.


These settings offer a relaxed and comfortable sense of
community featuring outstanding clubhouses,
incredible residential offerings,premier golf courses,all
offering highest of service standards to enrich your experience.

The Discovery Difference

Focus on family

Our properties have always been inspired by and created for families. We build settings that are as welcoming to everyone as they are exclusive.

Incomparable experiences

World-renowned golf courses, state-of-the-art fitness facilities, personalized outdoor concierge programs – our communities deliver unmatched amenities and service for all members of the family.

Inspired by land

Each property respectfully integrates the local architecture, culture, and cuisine to create an authentic and immersive experience.

Sustainable future

Through a focus on sustainable and regenerative efforts, we create programs that ensure we leave a positive footprint on the land.

Luxury bespoke homes

Through our in-house Discovery Builders and Discovery Creative we offer luxury turn-key homes for families that embody modern aesthetics while staying true to the classic principles of design.
